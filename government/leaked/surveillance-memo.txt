MINISTRY OF INTERIOR - INTERNAL MEMORANDUM
CLASSIFICATION: TOP SECRET
DATE: 2084-03-01

TO: All District Surveillance Coordinators
FROM: Minister of Interior
RE: PANOPTICON System Expansion

The PANOPTICON surveillance system expansion is proceeding on schedule.
Phase 3 implementation begins next month with the following enhancements:

- Facial recognition at all public transportation hubs
- Communication monitoring expanded to include encrypted channels
- Predictive analytics for identifying potential dissidents
- Automated alert system for suspicious behavior patterns

BUDGET ALLOCATION: 500 million credits
TIMELINE: Full deployment by 2084-06-01

District coordinators are reminded that this program is classified.
Any leaks will be investigated and prosecuted to the fullest extent.

All citizens should be considered potential threats until proven otherwise.
Trust no one.

-- Minister of Interior
