EMERGENCY PROCEDURES - MEMORIZE AND DESTROY

SCENARIO 1: SAFE HOUSE COMPROMISED
- Grab emergency kit (always packed and ready)
- Use emergency exit (marked with green tape)
- Rendezvous at backup location (see map in kit)
- Contact Command using emergency frequency
- Do not return to compromised location

SCENARIO 2: OPERATIVE CAPTURED
- Assume all information known to operative is compromised
- Change all codes, frequencies, and dead drop locations
- Alert all cells to go dark for 72 hours
- Prepare for increased surveillance and sweeps
- Do not attempt rescue without Command authorization

SCENARIO 3: COMMAND COMPROMISED
- All cells operate independently
- Cell leaders assume command of their areas
- Coordinate through pre-established backup channels
- Continue resistance operations at reduced capacity
- Await reconstitution of central command

SCENARIO 4: MASS ARRESTS / GOVERNMENT CRACKDOWN
- All operatives go underground immediately
- Destroy all physical evidence of resistance activity
- Use only dead drops for communication
- Reduce operations to intelligence gathering only
- Wait for all-clear signal before resuming

GENERAL RULES:
- Trust your training
- Protect your fellow operatives
- Never give up information under interrogation
- The cause is greater than any individual
- Stay strong. Stay free.
